About Me


I am a Postdoctoral Scholar at Stanford University. My research focuses on Artificial Intelligence applied to Biomedicine and Healthcare.

I hold a PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Purdue University and a Bachelor's degree in Telecommunications Engineering at Polytechnic University of Catalonia. During my PhD, I was a researcher at Purdue University, Stanford University, Qualcomm, and HP Labs. Previously I worked as an iOS developer at Visual Engineering.
