About Me


I am a Postdoctoral Scholar at Stanford University. My research focuses on Artificial Intelligence applied to Biomedicine and Healthcare. I hold a PhD from Purdue University and a Bachelor's degree from Polytechnic University of Catalonia. Previously, I worked at Purdue University, Stanford University, Qualcomm, HP Labs, and Visual Engineering.
