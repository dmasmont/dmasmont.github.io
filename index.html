About Me


I am the Director of Artificial Intelligence at Galatea Bio and a Research Affiliate at Stanford University. My work spans multiple fields including Artificial Intelligence, Population Genetics, Precision Medicine, Bioinformatics, Computer Vision, and MLOps. I am, or have been, affiliated with Galatea Bio, Stanford University, Purdue University, Polytechnic University of Catalonia, Qualcomm, HP Labs, Visual Engineering.
